SFDC stands for Salesforce.com, which is a cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) and agentic AI platform. It is one of the most popular and widely used CRM software platforms in the market, and is used by businesses of all sizes to manage their customer data, sales processes, marketing campaigns, and customer service activities with the power of AI agents and unified data. The acronym SFDC is often used as a shorthand for Salesforce.com in discussions, literature, and online forums related to the software.
